About Portfolio Generator

The Portfolio Generator is a web application designed to help users create and manage their online portfolios. Whether you are a student, job seeker, freelancer, or a professional, having an online portfolio can significantly enhance your visibility and showcase your skills and accomplishments to potential clients or employers.

With the Portfolio Generator, you can easily create a personalized portfolio by providing your personal data, skills, educational background, hobbies, and more. The application also allows you to set your proficiency level in various skills using sliders, making it visually appealing and interactive for visitors.

The Portfolio Generator is developed using a combination of front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the Bootstrap framework for responsive design. The back-end is powered by PHP, allowing seamless data processing and management. The application also uses a MySQL database to store user information securely.

Our team of talented developers and designers is committed to providing you with the best experience in creating your portfolio. We continuously strive to improve the application, adding new features and functionality based on user feedback and requirements.

Start building your online portfolio today with Portfolio Generator and take your professional presence to new heights!
